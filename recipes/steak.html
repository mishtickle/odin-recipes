Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt to flavour

Instructions

  1. Whirl it up and put it together
  2. Pour some oil into the frying pan
  3. Sear the steak on a high heat on both sides in a skillet
  4. Reduce the temperature to about half
  5. Rub in the salt
  6. Wait until cooked medium-rare - the juices should flow clear
