Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Spring Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine Vinegar

Instructions

Chop up lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes and spring onion

Mix together in a salad bowl

Add olive oil and red wine vinegar, and mix some more

Enjoy a nice healthy salad, good for those long summer days
